I'm a software developer and **Co-Founder of [LATAMWare](https://latamware.com)**, a company focused on **custom software development and intelligent automation** for businesses across Latin America.

I combine strong engineering fundamentals with a product and business mindset — caring not just about clean code, but about building things that actually move the needle.
